About

Hello! I'm Aerin, I use she/her pronouns, and I'm a Filipinx-American on occupied Coast Salish/Duwamish land.

Student and Programmer focused on Decolonized and Justice-Centered CS Applications

I'm currently a 3rd year student at the University of Washington majoring in Computer Science with a minor in Diversity. I'm passionate in centering a decolonized, justice-oriented approach to computer science and its applications. I hope to utilize my technical skillset to center the intersections of race, gender, class, and tech and work towards the liberation of all marginalized peoples.

I have experience in back-end development and am skilled in Java, C++, and more. When I’m not binge watching my favorite shows, organizing in my communities, or drinking obscene amounts of coffee, I’m napping, on TikTok, or both :)

UW BSU Demands

"The University of Washington prides itself on diversity which barely exist at the institution. After numerous conversations between President Ana Marie Cauce and the Black Student Union about our experiences and how we can better improve the diversity at this university, President Cauce has overlooked our experiences and refuses to take the actions necessary to making BIPOC students feel safe and welcome on campus. We have had enough. Thus Black Students will work together with faculty, allies and local activist to ensure that our demands are met."
