Projects

stud-e

Project Date: January 2020 - March 2020

stud-e is a personalized study assistant that records and analyzes student productivity information from previous study sessions and learns from that data to help users optimize study time and promote good study habits.

Completed in CSE 440 (Introduction to Human-Computer Interaction) in a group of four. Through the duration of this project, our team implemented the iterative design process to try and find a way to optimize study time, given this age of personal technology and the distractions that come with it. We participated in design research (user interviews), task design, storyboarding, paper prototyping, and digital prototyping.


Z-tionary

Project Date: October 2019

Z-tionary is a web application that takes text input (i.e. a phrase or sentence) and outputs a "translation" with all the identified Generation Z slang replaced with more easily understandable synonyms. Implemented in a team of four as part of DubHacks 2019. Used Firebase to host website and store database that maps Gen Z slang keys to dictionary English values. Backend developed in Java with frontend developed in HTML5/CSS.
